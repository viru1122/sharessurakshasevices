How to Check if Your Shares Are in IEPF – Complete Guide

March 15, 2026 5 min read By Sharesuraksha Team

If you've lost track of your physical share certificates or haven't received dividends for a long time, your shares may have been transferred to the Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF). Here's how to check.

What is IEPF?

The IEPF is a fund established by the government where shares and dividends that remain unclaimed for 7 consecutive years are transferred. You can still claim them back!

Step-by-Step Guide to Check IEPF Website

Step 1: Visit IEPF Website

Go to www.iepf.gov.in and click on "Search Unclaimed and Unpaid Amounts"

Step 2: Enter Your Details

You can search using:

  • Your Name
  • Folio Number (if you have old certificates)
  • Company Name
  • PAN Card (for recent claims)

Step 3: Review Results

If your shares are found, you'll see details like company name, folio number, and amount. Note down this information.

Step 4: Contact Us for Claim Assistance

Once you've identified your shares, we can help you with the claim process. We handle all paperwork and coordination with companies.

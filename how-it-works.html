How It Works

Simple 4-step process to recover your unclaimed shares and investments

1

You Submit Your Details

Fill out our free eligibility check form with your basic information. Share any old documents you have like:

  • Old share certificates (even if damaged)
  • Folio numbers
  • Dividend warrants
  • Company names where you held shares
Don't worry if you don't have documents – we can still search using your name and PAN!
2

We Verify & Locate Your Assets

Our team conducts a thorough search across multiple databases:

  • IEPF Database: Check for shares transferred to Investor Education and Protection Fund
  • Company Records: Contact company registrars for unclaimed dividends
  • Physical Share Registry: Locate lost physical share certificates
  • Ancestral Property Records: For inheritance claims
3

We Handle All Paperwork

Once assets are located, we prepare and file all required documents:

  • Form IEPF-5: For claiming shares from IEPF
  • Indemnity Bond: Legal protection for lost certificates
  • Affidavits: For name/signature mismatches
  • Succession Certificate: For legal heir cases
  • Transmission Requests: For transferring shares to your name

You just need to sign – we coordinate with company registrars and IEPF authority!

4

You Get Paid

After successful verification and approval:

  • Shares are credited directly to your Demat account
  • Dividends are transferred to your bank account
  • Physical shares are dematerialized (if needed)
You pay only after successful recovery! No recovery, no fee.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the recovery process take?

Typically 3-6 months, depending on the complexity and company response times. IEPF claims usually take 2-3 months after verification.

What if I don't have any documents?

No problem! We can search using your name, PAN, and old address. Many shares are recovered without any original documents.

What are your charges?

We work on a success fee basis – you pay only after we recover your shares. Our fee is a percentage of the recovered amount, agreed upon before starting.

Can you help with shares in my ancestor's name?

Yes! We specialize in legal heir claims and transmission of shares. We'll help you get the necessary succession certificate and file all paperwork.
